Shop/Store Database Management Software

This management software for shops and stores is a multi-function software.

Some of the functions of this software are...

    1. For selling items and products (eg. phones, laptops, bags, drugs, milk, bags of rice, etc)
    2. Keeping workers' records
    3. Keeping enrolment and attendance of workers
    4. Keeping shop information
    5. Tracking workers.
    6. Helps in the Preparation of annual accounts for the store/shop
    7. Have inbuilt calculator
    8. Shows the financial position of the firm.
    9. Inbuilt app for checking profit.
